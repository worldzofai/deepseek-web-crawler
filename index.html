Our Story

A Century
of Coffee

In the winter of 1924, Jan Kaffe opened the doors of this café on Malá Strana, Prague — a refuge for writers, artists, and philosophers seeking warmth and extraordinary coffee.

A century on, we carry that same spirit. The original Viennese chairs, the hand-painted ceiling, the smell of fresh roast mingling with history — none of it has changed. Only the coffee has gotten better.
